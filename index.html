What is HOW Conf?

HOW Conf is a one-day event chock full of hands-on workshops.

We want you to learn something new, and get involved in the action. Sessions may or may not be related to coding.

Workshops

We'd love for you to present! If you have an idea for a workshop, send it to info@howconf.org.

Location

Mallet Lodge at Platte River State Park in Nebraska. It is located halfway between Omaha and Lincoln.

A Nebraska Games & Parks permit is required to enter the park. Daily passes are $5 and can be paid when you arrive.

Platte River is a beautiful park, so feel free to stick around after HOW Conf and hike the trails, play tennis, or anything else you'd like to do!

What should I bring?

A laptop for with which to do workshops. If anything else is required for a workshop, we'll let you know beforehand.

If you'd like, bring your phone and tweet at us with the #howconf hashtag.

What's included

  • Light breakfast
  • Lunch
